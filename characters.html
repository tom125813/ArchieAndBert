Our Heroes

01

Archie

Jack Russell Terrier

Archie is a spirited, energetic Jack Russell with an insatiable appetite for adventure. His boundless optimism and fearless curiosity often lead him — and his best friend Bert — into exciting (and sometimes chaotic) situations across the African savanna.

Brave Curious Loyal Energetic
02

Bert

Hippopotamus

Bert is a thoughtful, gentle hippo and the steady voice of reason in their friendship. Though more cautious than Archie, his warmth, wisdom, and big heart always help the two of them find their way through any challenge they face together.

Wise Kind Patient Protective
