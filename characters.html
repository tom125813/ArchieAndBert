Our Heroes

Archie

Jack Russell

Archie is a spirited and energetic Jack Russell with an insatiable appetite for adventure. His boundless energy and optimistic outlook often lead him and his best friend Bert into exciting (and sometimes chaotic) situations across the African savanna.

Brave Curious Loyal Energetic

Bert

Hippo

Bert is a thoughtful and gentle hippo who serves as the voice of reason in their friendship. While he may be more cautious than Archie, his wisdom and big heart always help them find their way through any challenge.

Wise Kind Patient Protective
