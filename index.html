How It All Began

When we began writing our first book "Archie & Bert: Chaos in the Savanna," we had no idea how readers would respond. Since then, the ideas have kept coming — and so has the encouragement.

What started as a story has grown into something much bigger, including the exciting idea of Fun School.

From the beginning, we've wanted to make something — or as much as possible — completely free. No subscriptions. No hidden fees. Just free. Accessible film and learning for everyone.

We are now working alongside educational professionals to explore how far this journey can take us — together.

Every penny of book profit is donated to Tormohun Rotary Club — based in Torquay, Devon, UK — working tirelessly to support both people and animals in need.

We are proud to support their work. And we are only just getting started.
