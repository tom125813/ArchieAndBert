About Archie & Bert

A story about stories — and what happens when you dare to share them

Our Story

When we began writing our first book, "Archie & Bert: Chaos in the Savanna," we had no idea how readers would respond. Since then, the ideas have kept coming — and so has the encouragement.

What started as a simple story has grown into something much bigger, including the exciting idea of Fun School — free educational video content for every child, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.

From the beginning, we've wanted to make something — or as much as possible — completely free. Accessible film and learning for everyone. We are now working alongside educational professionals to explore how far this journey can take us — together.

While we do charge for our books, every penny of profit is donated to charity — specifically to Tormohun Rotary Club, based in Torquay, Devon, UK. They work tirelessly to support both people and animals in need, and we are proud to support their work.

And we are only just getting started.
